Transcendence Pong is a multiplayer web game for registered users. Users must not attempt to disrupt games, impersonate others, bypass authentication, or abuse chat.

Game results are recorded when a match finishes or when a disconnected player fails to reconnect in time. Leaderboards are generated from stored match data.

Users are responsible for appropriate profile and chat content. Blocking tools are provided to reduce unwanted direct interaction.

Administrators may disable accounts, remove abusive content, or reset test data when needed to protect the service and other users.

The application is provided for educational project evaluation and may change as modules are completed or reviewed.
